Write n Blog is a theme made for parent bloggers such as mommy and daddy blogs. Tell the hones truth about being a father or mother to your kids / baby / babies and let the world know that it's not always as good as it looks on Instagram. Create posts and stories about your maternity leave and pregnancy. Write n Blog is based on the theme Draftly, it offers a good amount of customization. The theme is a perfect combination of beautiful and professionalism. Our theme is made for the search engines, so you can easily beat the other bloggers and newspapers in Google with our fast and SEO optimized theme.
